More about Binh Minh hotel

Featuring 3-star accommodation, Bình Minh hotel is situated in Phụng Hiệp, 32 km from Ninh Kieu Pier and 32 km from Vincom Plaza Hung Vuong. The property is set 32 km from Can Tho Museum, 33 km from Can Tho Stadium and 32 km from Ninh Kieu Footbridge. The property is non-smoking and is located 30 km from Vincom Plaza Xuan Khanh. Featuring a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries, certain rooms at the hotel also offer a city view. Guest rooms will provide guests with a desk and a kettle. Can Tho International Airport is 39 km away.
